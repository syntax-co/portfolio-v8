


Cutthroat Country Club (CCC) is an exclusive investment community built on the ethereum blockchain, 
offering membership through the ownership of a randomly generated cutthroat old man NFT. This NFT 
serves as a profile picture and provides voting rights within the club. The CCC aims to amass wealth 
by utilizing community funds to invest in various assets, including NFT projects, cryptocurrencies, 
and traditional assets. Active Cutthroats receive daily rewards in the form of $STONKS tokens for a 
period of 10 years.
